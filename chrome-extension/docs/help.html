Recording a Macro

Click "Start Recording"
Navigate to the Macros tab and click the "Start Recording" button.
Perform Actions
Perform the actions you want to automate: clicks, typing, scrolling, navigation, etc.
Stop Recording
Click "Stop Recording" when finished. Your macro will be saved automatically.
Play It Back
Select your macro from the list and click "Play" to replay the actions.

üìÅ P2P File Sharing

How P2P Sharing Works

CUBE Nexum's P2P feature allows you to share files directly with other users without uploading to a server. All transfers are encrypted and peer-to-peer.

Setting Up P2P

Go to P2P Tab
Click the P2P icon in the navigation bar.
Note Your Peer ID
Copy your unique Peer ID and share it with the person you want to connect with.
Set Directories
Configure your Share Directory (files to share) and Download Directory (where to save received files).
Connect
Enter your peer's ID and click "Connect to Peer".

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Why isn't the floating assistant showing up?
The floating assistant may be disabled in settings, or the page may have security restrictions. Try refreshing the page or checking Settings > Show Floating Assistant.
Can I use CUBE on any website?
CUBE works on most websites, but some sites with strict Content Security Policies may limit functionality. Chrome internal pages (chrome://) are also restricted.
Are my macros stored securely?
Yes, macros are stored locally in your browser's extension storage. They are never uploaded to external servers unless you explicitly export them.
How do I update the extension?
Chrome automatically updates extensions. You can also manually check for updates in chrome://extensions by enabling Developer Mode and clicking "Update".
Does the AI assistant require an API key?
The basic AI features work out of the box. For advanced features, you may need to configure an OpenAI API key in Settings.
